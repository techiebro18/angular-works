QUALITY CONTROL
As a marketplace our top priority is to make sure the products live up to their product description. When an item is sold it will undergo quality control at our office, before we ship it to the buyer.

We have a team of brand experts who take every detail into account, methodically inspecting label, code, stamp, hardware, stitching, material, symmetry, and overall craftsmanship to ensure authenticity.
